Hôtel, Restaurant & Wellness

Welcome

A&B Hotel is a hotel establishment opened in 2022 by Mr Gorski Anthony & Mr Vandanjon Benoît.
This establishment was founded in a peaceful setting, in the heart of the beautiful town of Fontenay-le-Comte, where nature and landscape are in harmony. You will discover there a whole range of services and benefits in order to enjoy a relaxing stay.
Our restaurant "La bonne popote en remote", managed by renowned chef Mr Gorski, is also open throughout the year.

Spacious Bedroom

A set of rooms adapted to your needs

Our rooms have been designed with the aim of offering you the best possible comfort. Our bedding, voted best bedding of the year, will satisfy your sleep and give you a feeling of well-being.
Our rooms are spacious, bright and have a bathroom, a television area and a balcony.

Relaxing environement

A quiet environment for optimal relaxation

Our hotel, located in the beautiful town of Fontenay-le-Conte, offers you a dreamy and idyllic landscape. Enjoy a calm and relaxing environment to spend a moment of relaxation.
You will discover a set of walks and hikes close to our establishment. You will also find our beautiful Vendée beaches a few km away
